ITSM

IT Service Management (ITSM) is a strategic approach in IT that focuses on delivering value to customers through well-defined processes. It encompasses activities like incident management, change management, problem management, and service request fulfillment. ITSM aims to align IT services with business needs, ensuring efficient and reliable operations. By standardizing processes, ITSM improves service quality, enhances user satisfaction, and supports continuous improvement in IT service delivery.
